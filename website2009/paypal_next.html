Paying National, Regional, and Chapter Dues
via UA NSBE Website

$20 payment covers National ($10), Regional ($3), and Chapter ($7) dues.
  1. Logon to NSBE Account.
    • If you are a new NSBE member, create a profile on NOL. If you have been a NSBE member, login and update your profile information.
  2. Select "Collegiate" membership type ($10-National, $3-Regional - all online).
  3. Once you have completed and/or updated your profile, NOL will ask you for payment. Select the "Pay Later/Chapter Payment" option.
  4. After completing the transaction on NSBE Account, return to this page.
  5. Select the "National, Regional, and Chapter Membership $20.00" option and click the "Buy Now" button.
  6. Either fill in the requested payment information or login with your Paypal account and complete the payment transaction.
  7. IMPORTANT! After completing all the previous steps, PLEASE notify the UA NSBE Treasurer of your payment, to make sure that it will be credited on your behalf.

Paying ONLY Chapter Dues via UA NSBE Website

$7 payment covers Chapter dues.
  1. Logon to NSBE Account.
    • If you are a new NSBE member, create a profile on NOL. If you have been a NSBE member, login and update your profile information.
  2. Select "Collegiate" membership type ($10-National, $3-Regional - all online).
  3. Once you have completed and/or updated your profile, NOL will ask you for payment. Select the "Credit Card" option and fill in the requested information.
  4. After completing the transaction on NSBE Account, return to this page.
  5. Select the "Chapter ONLY Membership $7.00" option and click the "Buy Now" button.
  6. Either fill in the requested payment information or login with your Paypal account and complete the payment transaction.
  7. IMPORTANT! After completing all the previous steps, PLEASE notify the UA NSBE Treasurer of your payment, to make sure that it will be credited on your behalf.
