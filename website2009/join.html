How do I become a member of NSBE?

  • You can join by paying your $20 membership dues ($10 National, $3 Regional, $7 Chapter) either
    • Preferred: online via credit card, debit card, or PayPal. View the online payment options.

      OR

    • in person to the UA NSBE Treasurer. Email the UA NSBE Treasurer to setup a time to meet.

Top of Page


What do I get in return as being a
member of NSBE?

  • Discounts available through various companies.
  • Benefits include scholarships, grants, and leadership skills.
  • Professional conferences across the region and nation.
  • An addition to your resume that is well-known by top companies.
  • Free Microsoft software provided through the MSDN Academic Alliance.

Top of Page


What do I get in return as being a
member of UA NSBE?

  • Access to the UA NSBE/MEP Test Bank. The Test Bank includes tests, homework assignments, and more from past students that have taken the classes that you are currently taking or will have to take in the future.
  • Gain Community Service by helping tutor students at community schools and the local branch of the YMCA.
