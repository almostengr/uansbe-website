Event Descriptions

Fundraising Events

  • Basketball Tournament
    Teams enter the tournament to compete against each other to win a cash prize. This event is usually held during the Spring semester.
  • Bake Sale
    UA NSBE members bake goods, including cookies and cake, and sell them to students passing through the student center and other common gathering places.
  • Car Washes
    A frequent event held throughout the academic year on Saturdays. UA NSBE parters with a local business to hand wash vehicles in effort to raise money for the chapter.
  • Date Auction
    An annual fundraising event where potential dates are auctioned off. The participants are not obligated to go on a date with the bidder. Each bidder will receive a gift for helping out. Gifts range from different value amounts of restaurant coupons to a free meal or two. The most popular gift is wing party for 10 at Hooters (this is awarded to the highest bidder). So please come out and enjoy the night. Refreshments will be available in the VIP section (VIP includes participants in Date Auction and winning bidders).
  • Halloween Carnival
    We normally have a Fall Carnival, but this year we decided to try something different. There will be a costume party, but you are not required to wear a costume. There will be rewards given to the top 3 costumes. So it might be worth your while to dress up. Of course some party refreshments.

Events and Activities

  • General Body Meetings
    Meeting with all the members of the UA NSBE chapter. Current members are encourage to bring and invite new members to these meetings. Guest speakers are invited to deliver information about their company or organization. Also, members are brought up to date with current news and upcoming event info.
  • Back To School BBQ
    An event held at the beginning of the academic year in conjuction with UAT-NSBE Jr. Chapter.
  • Networking and Sponsorship Banquet
    A formal event where students that are majoring in all technical majors at the University of Alabama are given the opportunity to network with potential employers over dinner.
  • End of Year Banquet
    This event is for NSBE Members only. This is when we pass the Torch from old officers to new officers. Also during this event, any and all other awards will be handed out to the Chapter's most active and influential members.
  • Fun Night
    This is a free event that is open campus wide. Fun Night is simply a chance for he student body to get together and share poems, jokes, dance, whatever. The limits are endless as long as you try really hard to keep it clean. It is an outlet for you. No judges, no awards, just a night of fun!
  • Joint Meetings with other engineering organizations
    Meetings are jointly held with other engineering societies including American Society of Mechanical Engineers (ASME) and Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers (SHPE).
  • Professional Fashion Show
    This is a campus wide event where we will present to the students the Do's and Don'ts when dressing for the business world. We will show you the correct way to wear business casual as well.
  • Study Break
    An event held during Dead Week (the week before Finals) to give members a stress-free enviroment from studying with fun, games, and socializing.

Top of Page

Other Programs

  • UAT (University of Alabama Tuscaloosa)-NSBE Jr. Chapter
    View UAT-NSBE Jr. chapter being featured on the NSBE PCI website.
  • T.E.A.M. (Teaching Executive Administration to Members)
    This program gives the opportunity for members to shadow current Chapter Executive Board members to see how a NSBE chapter should function and be organized.
    UA NSBE T.E.A.M. on Facebook
  • Community Service
    UA NSBE members tutor younger students at the T.O.R.C.H. Center and coach sports teams at the Tuscaloosa Branch of the YMCA.
  • Intramural Sports Teams
    UA NSBE members and other University students participate in the University's Intramural Sports League. The teams include Flag Football, Volleyball, Basketball, and Softball.
